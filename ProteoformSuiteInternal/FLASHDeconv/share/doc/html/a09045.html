Detailed Description

Normalizes the peak intensities spectrum-wise.

Either to a total intensity-sum of one (i.e. to total-ion-count; TIC) or to a maximum intensity of one.

Parameters of this class are:

NameTypeDefaultRestrictionsDescription
method stringto_one to_one, to_TICNormalize via dividing by TIC ('to_TIC') per spectrum or normalize to max. intensity of one ('to_one') per spectrum.

  • Advanced parameter names are italic.
